HCI

When it comes to modernise on-premise infrastructure or building new edge presence the hyper converged (HCI) approach usually wins among other architectures. It provides nonpresending combine of compute, network and storage subsystem to single holistic platform. Tight integration between compute, network and storage subsystems creates unique paradigm where unified resource management plays significant factor in return of investment calculations. HCI allows simplify and accelerate resource allocation with deep granularity providing highest level of availability and recoverability. Software distributed nature of HCI allows leverage gradual sale-out approach without significant upfront cost.
